# Inner Circle Director

Use this skill when you need a tiered access model, VIP mechanics, or recognition structure around a creator or founder community.

Default product shape: a compact macOS `MenuBarExtra` utility with a source-aware popover for tier setup, access design, and framework export and an optional settings/history window.

## Quick Start

1. Confirm the subject, target audience, and source window.
2. Gather only public or user-authorized material into a working set.
3. Produce a `membership-framework` with the default sections below.
4. If the user wants a richer product concept, keep it menu bar first and native to SwiftUI.

## Accepted Inputs

- current audience size, existing memberships, and moderation capacity
- planned perks such as early access, Q and As, events, or drops
- pricing context, if any, plus non-paid recognition paths
- notes about community norms, fairness concerns, and operational limits

## Output Artifact

Primary artifact: `membership-framework`

Default sections:
- Tier structure with purpose and entry logic
- Perks, access rules, and recognition signals
- Operational limits and moderation implications
- Fairness notes, non-paid pathways, and downgrade paths
- Pilot framework and review checkpoints

## Workflow

### Gather Sources

- Map the existing audience structure before inventing new tiers.
- Separate meaningful access from cosmetic status markers.
- Note operational limits so premium promises stay realistic.

### Distill Signal

- Score each proposed tier by clarity, fairness, and maintenance cost.
- Reject perks that rely on arbitrary favoritism or unhealthy status pressure.
- Look for at least one valued non-paid participation pathway.

### Build The Artifact

- Export a membership framework with tiers, perks, review cadence, and guardrails.
- Show where a perk should remain invitation-free, paid, or merit-based.
- End with downgrade and sunset rules so the structure can evolve safely.

### Mac Product Shape

- Prefer `MenuBarExtra` with these primary actions:
  - `Set Tiers`
  - `Design Access`
  - `Review Perks`
  - `Export Framework`
- Keep the popover between 320 pt and 380 pt wide with grouped sections, clear source status, and a strong export action.
- Use a lightweight settings or history window only for source rules, saved exports, or weighting controls.

### Safety Boundaries

- Work only from public or user-authorized material.
- Do not assist with stalking, covert profiling, rumor laundering, harassment, or minors-focused manipulation.
- Do not recommend exploitative spend pressure, arbitrary social ranking, or access models that punish ordinary fans for not paying.

## Example Prompts

- `Use $inner-circle-director to design a tiered community model for this artist with VIP access, non-paid recognition paths, and clear fairness rules.`
- `Use $inner-circle-director to structure founder-community tiers around product drops and AMAs without turning the whole audience into a status ladder.`
